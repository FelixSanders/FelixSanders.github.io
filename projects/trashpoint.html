TrashPoint

A reward-based app that motivates students to collect and recycle trash.

TrashPoint is a gamified environmental awareness app designed to encourage students to collect and recycle trash. Each item collected earns points, which can be exchanged for rewards. The project aims to foster sustainable habits and make cleanliness a fun, rewarding activity within schools.

Built using: Kodular
